MoSKito Commiters

Fingers and Brains behind MoSKito

MoSKito is an open source project and it is developed entirely by volunteers.

Want to help? Jump to the help section to learn how you can involve yourself.

Maintainer

The project is maintained by the Maintainer. The job of the maintainer is to

  • discuss and accept pull requests on GitHub,
  • prescan and assign jira tickets
  • prepare and perform releases
  • ask new commiters to fill out and sign the Contributor License Agreement.

    MoSKito's maintainer and founder is:
    Leon Rosenberg

    You can always reach current maintainer at maintainer@moskito.org

Want to help?

MoSKito is an open source project maintained by volunteers. This means, that we urgently depend on your help.

There are multiple ways to help. Here are some:

  • Check jira for unresolved issues.
  • Check how MoSKito integrates with your favorite tech and provide an example or documentation or a blog post.
  • Generally everything that makes the documentation better, will give you hundreds of additional karma points.
  • Or maybe you have a feature idea? Discuss it on the user list.
  • ..... or maybe just send us a pizza ;-)
